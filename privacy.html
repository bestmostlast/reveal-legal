Privacy Policy for REVEAL: Detective Mysteries

Last Updated: April 29, 2026 Effective Date: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

REVEAL: Detective Mysteries (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Game Data (Stored Locally)

  • Game progress: Cases solved, levels completed, daily challenge history
  • User preferences: Language selection, music/SFX toggle settings
  • Profile information: Detective avatar selection, nickname (optional)

Where it’s stored: On your device only How long: Until you uninstall the app or delete your profile Shared with: No third parties (stored locally on device)

2.2 Device Information (Sent to Servers)

  • Device type: iPhone, iPad, or Android device model
  • Operating system version: iOS 14+, Android 7.0+
  • App version: Current version of REVEAL installed
  • Unique Device ID (UUID): Anonymous identifier (not tied to your name or email)

Where it’s stored: Firebase Crashlytics database How long: 30 days, then automatically deleted Shared with: Google Firebase (Google LLC)

2.3 Crash Reports (Automatic)

If the app crashes, we automatically send: - Stack trace: Technical information about the crash - Device info: OS, device model, app version - Timestamp: When the crash occurred

Purpose: To identify and fix bugs Where it’s stored: Firebase Crashlytics Shared with: Google Firebase (crash analysis)

2.4 Analytics (Optional)

If enabled, we track: - Screen views: Which screens you visit in the app - Button taps: Which features you use most - Session duration: How long you play per session - Leaderboard entries: Your detective name and points (public)

Purpose: To understand user behavior and improve the app Where it’s stored: Google Analytics Shared with: Google Analytics (Google LLC) Optional: This is optional—users can opt-out in settings

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your real name
  • ❌ Your email address
  • ❌ Your phone number
  • ❌ Your location
  • ❌ Your contacts
  • ❌ Your photos (except avatar selection)
  • ❌ Your payment information (handled by Apple/Google)

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Firebase (Crashlytics)

  • Purpose: Crash reporting and app stability
  • Data sent: Device info, crash logs, app version
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Data location: Google servers (US and EU)

3.2 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • Purpose: Serving advertisements in the app
  • Data sent: Device type, language, general region (not exact location)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-out: Users can remove ads for $9.99

3.3 Google Play Services

  • Purpose: In-app purchases, sign-in, game services
  • Data sent: Transaction history, authentication info
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.4 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: In-app purchases, app delivery
  • Data sent: Purchase history, device ID
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4. Data Retention & Deletion

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Game progress: Until you uninstall or delete account
  • Crash reports: 30 days (automatically deleted)
  • Analytics: 14-24 months (configurable in Google Analytics)
  • Leaderboard entries: Until app removal or account deletion

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Delete game progress:
    • Open app → Settings → Clear Game Data
    • All local progress deleted immediately
    • Leaderboard entry removed within 24 hours
  2. Request complete data deletion:
    • Email: bestmostlast@gmail.com
    • Subject: “Data Deletion Request - [Your Device ID]”
    • We will delete all records within 30 days
  3. Stop crash reporting:
    • Settings → Privacy → Crash Reports → Toggle OFF
    • No new crash data will be sent
    • Existing reports deleted per Firebase policy

Your Rights: - Right of Access: Request a copy of your data - Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data - Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”) - Right to Data Portability: Receive data in a portable format - Right to Object: Opt-out of data processing

How to Exercise Your Rights: Email: bestmostlast@gmail.com with your request and device ID

Data Processing Basis: - Legitimate interest (improve app quality, prevent fraud) - User consent (optional analytics, ads) - Legal obligation (required crash reporting for app stability)

For users in California:

Your Rights: - Right to Know: What personal information is collected - Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data - Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of “sale” of personal data

How to Exercise Your Rights: Email: bestmostlast@gmail.com with subject “California Privacy Rights”

Note: We do NOT “sell” personal information in the traditional sense. We may share analytics data with Google, which could be considered a “sale” under California law. You can opt-out by disabling analytics in Settings.
